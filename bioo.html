Ajim Biography:
(1991-)

who is ajim?

Ajim is an IT professional currently working as an Automation Test Engineer at AmeriHealth Caritas Family of Companies located at Philadelphia, PA.

Early Life

Ajim was born in 1991, in Narsingdi, Bangladesh. He grew up in an upper-middle-class family with his older sisters, Rani, Lima, and Sima in his hometown. Their father, Kaykobad Khandaker (Konak- passes away on May 10, 2017), was a promising, if somewhat shy, H.S.C student when he met his future wife, Spephaly Begum.
The Ajim family atmosphere was warm and close, and all four children were encouraged to be competitive and strive for excellence.
He completed his Bachelor in Engineering on April, 2012, and strated his carrier in Ohio, USA. He got married with Dr. Mily on Oct 18, 2018.
